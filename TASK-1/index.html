About Marvel Cinematic Universe

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a multimedia franchise created by Marvel Studios. It is based on characters from Marvel Comics, featuring a sprawling narrative of interconnected films and television series. It began with the release of Iron Man in 2008 and has since grown into one of the highest-grossing franchises in cinematic history.
